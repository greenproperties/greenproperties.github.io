BACKGROUND

Edward (Ward) J. Green, Jr. is President of Green Commercial Properties, Inc. He has been active in commercial real estate marketing and brokerage for over 20 years. His focus is on Tenant Representation, specializing in office, office/service and industrial commercial real estate. He is a Life Member of the Atlanta Commercial Board of Realtors Million Dollar Club. In 2000, he received the Phoenix Award from the Atlanta Commercial Board of Realtors, in recognition of 10 years of membership in the Million Dollar Club. He has also earned the Certified Commercial Investment Member Designation (CCIM).


Ward is a native of Mobile, Alabama. He graduated from Hampden-Sydney College,Hampden-Sydney, Virginia with a Bachelor of Arts degree in History. Ward and his wife Meg, live in Roswell, Georgia with their three children.
